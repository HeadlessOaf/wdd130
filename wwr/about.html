History

White Water Rafting was founded in 2025 by Mario Moya, a passionate adventurer and rafting enthusiast. With a vision to provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences, Mario started this company to share his love for the great outdoors with others. Over the years, White Water Rafting has grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for its commitment to safety, quality, and unforgettable adventures.
